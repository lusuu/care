Diabetes mellitus usually occurs in middle-aged to older dogs. Any breed can be affected but some are more predisposed than others.

Signs associated with diabetes may include:

  • Increased drinking
  • Increased urinating
  • Increased appetite
  • Weight loss
  • Dehydration
  • Infections – urinary tract, skin, lungs
  • Vomiting, diarrhoea, halitosis
  • Lethargy
  • Poor eyesight due to cataracts
